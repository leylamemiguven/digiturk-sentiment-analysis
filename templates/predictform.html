Sentiment Analysis (SA) aims to determine the attitude of a speaker, writer, or other subject with respect to some topic or the overall contextual polarity or emotional reaction to a document, interaction, or event. Binary sentiment classification includes positive and negative class while multi-class sentiment classification consists of more than two classes, i.e., positive, highly positive, negative, highly negative and neutral etc.
